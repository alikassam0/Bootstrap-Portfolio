About Me

I was born in Dallas, TX but grew up in Richmond Virginia. I graduated from George Mason University in 2010 with a degree in Economics. I currently work as a Quality Assurance Analyst for United States Citizenship and Immigration Services, and I love my job and the people I work with

I tend to stay busy in my "free" time. I attend Grace Church in Arlington. I'm part of the leadership team for the young adults ministry, which means I get to coordinate lots of fun events. I also volunteer through the church. I highly value living a healthy lifestyle. As such, I enjoy going to the gym and cooking healthy. I especially enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, running, and kayaking.
